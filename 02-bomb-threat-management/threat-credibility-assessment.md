# Threat Credibility Assessment

## Purpose
To evaluate the seriousness of a bomb threat and determine the appropriate response level.

## Credibility Indicators
- Specific details provided (time, location, device)
- Caller knowledge of the facility
- Consistency of information
- Emotional tone and urgency
- Current events or grievances

## Credibility Levels

| Level | Description | Recommended Action |
|------|-------------|--------------------|
| High | Specific and credible details | Immediate evacuation and police response |
| Medium | Some details but unverified | Partial evacuation, investigation |
| Low | Vague or implausible | Monitor, document, increase awareness |

## Important Notes
- Final decisions should involve senior management and police
- Document assessment rationale clearly
