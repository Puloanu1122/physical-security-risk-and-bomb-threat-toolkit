## Purpose
This document establishes a structured approach for managing bomb threats to ensure safety, effective decision-making, and coordination with authorities.

## What Is a Bomb Threat
A bomb threat is any communication indicating the possible presence of an explosive device. Threats may be received via phone, email, letter, social media, or through discovery of a suspicious object.

## Objectives
- Protect life and critical assets
- Ensure calm and controlled response
- Enable informed evacuation decisions
- Support law enforcement operations

## Threat Types
- Telephone threat
- Written or electronic threat
- Suspicious package or device
- Indirect or third-party threat

## Key Principles
- Treat all threats as credible until assessed
- Do not touch suspicious objects
- Avoid panic and uncontrolled evacuation
- Maintain clear communication and documentation
