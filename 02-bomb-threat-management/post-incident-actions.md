# Post-Incident Actions

## Purpose
To provide a structured procedure after a bomb threat or false alarm, ensuring safety, documentation, and lessons learned.

## Immediate Actions
1. Confirm all personnel are safe and accounted for
2. Ensure the facility is secure before re-entry
3. Coordinate with law enforcement for post-incident inspection
4. Identify any suspicious items or damages

## Documentation
- Complete a detailed **incident report**
- Include:
  - Date and time of threat
  - Threat type and credibility assessment
  - Actions taken (evacuation, lockdown, authorities notified)
  - Observations and any evidence collected
- Store logs in `/05-templates/incident-report-template.docx`

## Review and Debrief
1. Conduct a debrief with security team and management
2. Identify what went well and what could be improved
3. Update **SOPs and checklists** based on findings
4. Communicate lessons learned to relevant staff

## Follow-Up
- Repair or replace any compromised security systems
- Provide counseling or support to affected personnel
- Maintain records for compliance and future reference

## Notes
- Post-incident review is critical for continuous improvement
- Always comply with local regulations and law enforcement guidance
