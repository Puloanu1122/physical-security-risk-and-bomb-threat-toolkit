# Bomb Threat Call Checklist

## Purpose
Provides a structured set of questions and procedures for handling a bomb threat received via phone, ensuring accurate information gathering and appropriate response.

## Immediate Actions
1. Remain calm and courteous
2. Keep the caller on the line as long as possible
3. Do not interrupt unnecessarily
4. Notify a supervisor discreetly

## Caller Information to Collect
- **Date and Time of Call:**  
- **Caller’s Number/ID:**  
- **Caller’s Voice:** Male/Female, accent, tone, speech pattern  
- **Emotional State:** Calm, agitated, laughing, crying  
- **Background Sounds:** Traffic, machinery, music, children, sirens  

## Threat Details to Record
1. Exact words used by caller  
2. Claimed location of device  
3. Type of explosive or device mentioned  
4. Time of detonation  
5. Reason or motivation if provided  

## Recommended Actions
- Immediately log all details in **Bomb Threat Log** (`/05-templates/bomb-threat-log.docx`)  
- Do **not attempt to locate or touch the device**  
- Evacuate or restrict access based on threat credibility (`bomb-threat-overview.md`)  
- Coordinate with local authorities and emergency services  

## Notes
- Treat every threat as credible until proven otherwise  
- Keep communication records and preserve any voicemail, email, or message evidence
