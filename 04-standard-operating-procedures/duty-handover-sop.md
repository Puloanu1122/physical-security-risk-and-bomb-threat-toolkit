# Duty Handover SOP

## Purpose
To standardize the process of handing over and taking over security duty to ensure continuity, accountability, and clarity of responsibilities.

## Procedures

### 1. Taking Over Duty
- Arrive at least 10 minutes before shift start
- Review previous shiftâ€™s logbook or report
- Inspect security equipment (radios, CCTV, alarms)
- Confirm status of all posts and ongoing activities
- Brief with outgoing staff regarding incidents or special instructions

### 2. Handover Duty
- Provide incoming staff with a detailed briefing of incidents and observations
- Update logbook with any changes or issues during your shift
- Check and hand over all equipment
- Ensure the incoming staff acknowledges understanding

### 3. Documentation
- Complete a **Duty Handover Sheet** (`/05-templates/duty-handover-template.docx`)
- Record any follow-up actions required
- Maintain the logbook for management review

## Notes
- Clear communication is critical to prevent security gaps
- Any unusual observations should be highlighted and documented
- Maintain professionalism and adherence to SOP at all times
