# Patrol and Post Orders SOP

## Purpose
To standardize patrol routines and post assignments, ensuring consistent security presence and rapid incident detection.

## Procedures

### 1. Patrol Duties
- Conduct scheduled and random patrols of the facility
- Monitor entrances, exits, and sensitive areas
- Check for unusual or suspicious activity
- Report all findings to the control room

### 2. Post Orders
- Follow specific instructions for assigned posts (lobby, gates, restricted areas)
- Verify identification of all personnel and visitors
- Maintain communication with team leader and control room
- Document observations in the post log

### 3. Reporting
- Immediately report incidents or suspicious items
- Record all routine patrol checks in the patrol log
- Submit end-of-shift report to supervisor

## Notes
- Patrols should be unpredictable to deter unauthorized activity
- Maintain high visibility without compromising safety
- All staff must be familiar with emergency procedures
