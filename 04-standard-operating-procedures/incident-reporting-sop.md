# Incident Reporting SOP

## Purpose
To standardize the reporting of security incidents to ensure timely response, accountability, and record keeping.

## Procedures
1. **Immediate Notification**
   - Notify the supervisor or control room immediately after an incident occurs
   - For emergencies, call local authorities as required

2. **Gather Information**
   - Date, time, and location of incident
   - Parties involved (names, positions)
   - Detailed description of the incident
   - Witnesses and their contact information

3. **Documentation**
   - Complete the **Incident Report Template** (`/05-templates/incident-report-template.docx`)
   - Include photos or evidence if available
   - Submit the report within the timeframe set by management (typically within 24 hours)

4. **Follow-Up**
   - Review with security supervisor for verification
   - Implement corrective actions if necessary
   - Archive reports for future reference

## Notes
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
- Ensure reports are factual, clear, and concise
