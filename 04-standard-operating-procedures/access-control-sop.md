# Access Control SOP

## Purpose
To ensure that only authorized personnel access the facility, protecting people, assets, and information.

## Procedures
1. **Identification**  
   - All staff, visitors, and contractors must display ID badges at all times  
2. **Entry Authorization**  
   - Verify access level before granting entry  
   - Use visitor logbooks or electronic access systems  
3. **Escort Policy**  
   - Visitors without access credentials must be escorted by authorized personnel  
4. **Security Checkpoints**  
   - Random bag checks or screening may be conducted according to policy  
5. **Denial of Access**  
   - Unauthorized personnel must be politely denied entry and reported to supervisor

## Documentation
- Maintain daily access logs  
- Record all access denials and unusual incidents
