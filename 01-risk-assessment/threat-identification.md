# Threat Identification

## Purpose
Threat identification is the first step in physical security risk assessment. It focuses on identifying credible threats that may impact people, assets, and operations.

## Common Physical Security Threats
- Bomb threats and IEDs
- Unauthorized access and intrusion
- Theft and sabotage
- Insider threat
- Workplace violence
- Protest and civil disturbance

## Threat Sources
- Criminal actors
- Disgruntled employees or insiders
- Terrorist or extremist groups
- Opportunistic offenders
- Activist organizations

## Key Considerations
- Location and environment
- Facility profile and attractiveness
- Historical incidents
- Current threat intelligence
