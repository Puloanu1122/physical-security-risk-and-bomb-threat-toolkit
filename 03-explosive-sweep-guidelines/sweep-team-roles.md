# Sweep Team Roles

## Purpose
Defines clear roles and responsibilities for personnel involved in explosive sweep operations to ensure coordination, safety, and accountability.

## Sweep Team Composition

### 1. Team Leader
**Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate sweep operations
- Assign sweep areas and routes
- Maintain communication with management and authorities
- Ensure safety protocols are followed
- Authorize start and completion of sweeps

### 2. Sweep Operators
**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct systematic visual searches
- Identify suspicious or unattended items
- Maintain situational awareness
- Immediately report findings to the Team Leader
- Avoid touching or moving suspicious objects

### 3. Safety Observer
**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor team safety during sweeps
- Ensure safe distances are maintained
- Identify environmental hazards
- Stop sweep operations if unsafe conditions arise

### 4. Liaison Officer
**Responsibilities:**
- Communicate with law enforcement and emergency services
- Provide updates to facility management
- Coordinate access and information sharing

## Key Principles
- Clear communication at all times
- No individual operates alone
- All findings must be documented
- Authority decisions override operational pace
